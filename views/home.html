More About Us

As a small company taking on the role of providing high-quality equipment to hospitals and clinics, A.A. Bionics aims to continue producing technological advancements that give amputees a better quality of life. With an inspiring company culture and dedicated team focusing on Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion, A.A. Bionics drives the race for innovating improvements to automated prosthetics. Here at A.A. Bionics, we ensure that every employee is equally important to the team. Steady momentum is a direct result of our team cohesion. We like to keep the work environment here casual to give employees more independence and less supervision instead of the traditional hierarchical structure. By having fewer supervisors, we give employees more responsibility. As mentioned in this article, Hierarchical vs Organizational , more productivity is done when employees are given more freedom and power in their daily tasks. Additionally, we hold many events to involve the families of employees to build a better work/life balance.

We at A.A. Bionics ensure everyone is working to their potential while also taking the necessary precautions against being overworked. In fact, according to the CDC, productivity is greatly affected by the amount of rest employees get. More about this topic from the National Library of Medicine can be read here, Why Sleep Matters. We ensure our employees are taking regular breaks and vacations. In conjecture with our company mission to provide long-term care and prosthetic equipment, we also focus on long-term care for our own employees. A.A. Bionics will continue to work towards providing employees with a workplace that helps them grow and build themselves whether they work here for 20 years or five years.
