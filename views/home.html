About the Internship

Our internship provides base level knowledge on languages and applications, as well as standard procedures and practices for automations and prosthetic understandings. Throughout the training you will meet with your supervisor regularly to facilitate your success in meeting your professional goals.

More About Us

As a small company taking on the role of providing high-quality equipment to hospitals and clinics, A.A. Bionics aims to continue producing technological advancements that give amputees a better quality of life. With an inspiring company culture and dedicated team focusing on Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion, A.A. Bionics drives the race for innovating improvements to automated prosthetics. Here at A.A. Bionics, we ensure that every employee is equally important to the team. Steady momentum is a direct result of our team cohesion. We like to keep the work environment here casual to give employees more independence and less supervision instead of the traditional hierarchical structure. By having fewer supervisors, we give employees more responsibility. We believe more productivity is done when employees are given more freedom and power in their daily tasks. Additionally, we hold many events to involve the families of employees to build a better work/life balance.
